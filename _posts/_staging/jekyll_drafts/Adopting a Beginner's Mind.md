> Smart people learn from everything and everyone, average people from their experiences, stupid people already have all the answers - Socrates

  

+ ### Thoughts

+ Our ideas and opinions can be imprisoning.

+ Chill out, you don’t know so much.

+ And you know a few things that you won’t know that you know.

+ What you know can be seriously problematic.

+ It can anchor you to the known

+ and blind you from what is on the horizon that is not known.

+ Allow yourself to unblock from the deadlocks

+ And a new pathway forward to growth

+ Embrace the childlike curiosity & playfulness

  

[Who is my Inner Child?](craftdocs://open?blockId=4F218C26-9A4D-4653-922A-DE4156AE8801&spaceId=b287ed36-debf-9947-e267-c2ab9a4a94e4) If I cannot tap into my inner child and nestle it in my current age, I can at least adopt the traits of my childlike [Curiosity & Interest](craftdocs://open?blockId=317AD39D-5818-439E-9111-94F45D5FAFEE&spaceId=b287ed36-debf-9947-e267-c2ab9a4a94e4) to seek adventure and be playful in seeking out the wonders of the world, experiencing [Awe & Wonder](craftdocs://open?blockId=B5A06131-6F16-43A6-BF9F-76482F71CA6A&spaceId=b287ed36-debf-9947-e267-c2ab9a4a94e4) and living life!

  

+ Allow yourself to (re)validate your assumptions

+ Becoming an observer or in passive mode allows you to gather more data

+ “You have to be incredibly smart to ask dumb questions”

+ Be open to be treated like an idiot

+ Vuja De! Look at the situation from a fresh pair of eyes

+ Divergent thinking allows you to see way many more possibilities

+ You need to overcome your mental fixedness by letting go of your assumptions

+ Kill your ego and superiority,

+ be open to feedback and

+ bite the bullet when it comes to recognition.

+ Let others shine as you are focused on building yourself

+ and not getting accolades from others

+ to air Admiration & Reverence.

+ Embrace the feeling of Inferiority to be on the path of Mastery.
